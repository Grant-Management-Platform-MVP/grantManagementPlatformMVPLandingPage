Elevate Your Grant Management

Gain funder confidence and elevate your grant management by adopting the world's first and leading international standard for good financial grant practice and accountability.

Good Financial Grant Practice (GFGP) is a standard by ARSO that promotes standardized, accountable, and efficient grant management.

Benefits
  • Lower Costs: Cuts administrative overhead.
  • Fewer Audits: Avoids redundant reviews.
  • More Trust: Builds grantor confidence.
  • Less Risk: Reduces fraud and corruption.
  • Capacity Growth: Supports grantee development.

Tiers of Compliance:

GFGP supports organizations through four levels:

Bronze Silver Gold Platinum
  • Select Your Tier
    Organizations begin by selecting the GFGP tier they wish to assess against.
  • Complete Tier-Specific Assessments
    For each tier, the portal provides a comprehensive questionnaire corresponding to GFGP standard requirements, covering:
    • Financial Management: Planning and budgeting, income and expenditure management, asset management, cash and treasury, travel expenses, sub-grantee management, financial systems, and reporting.
    • Human Resources: HR management, payroll processes, and staff development.
    • Procurement: Planning and contract management.
    • Governance: Governance principles, grant management and compliance, audits, and risk management.
  • Upload Supporting Evidence
    Organizations can upload relevant documents (e.g., policies, procedure manuals, financial or audit reports) to support their responses.
  • Receive Automated Feedback and Gap Analysis
    After completing the assessment, the portal generates a report showing alignment with the selected GFGP tier and identifies gaps or areas for improvement.
  • Access Resources and Guidance
    The portal offers resources such as explanations of GFGP requirements, best practice examples, document templates, and links to the official ARSO GFGP standard.
  • Get Certified to GFGP
    A licensed GFGP Certification Body performs an independent on-site or remote audit to verify compliance with the chosen tier.
  • Track Progress
    Organizations can save results, conduct follow-ups, and track their progress toward improved alignment with GFGP standards.

By offering a structured and comprehensive self-evaluation process, the GFGP portal empowers organizations to proactively adopt good financial grant practices, enhance their accountability, and ultimately increase their effectiveness in managing grant funding.
